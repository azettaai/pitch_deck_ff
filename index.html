Detailed Results & Research Foundation

Mathematical Foundation

The YAT-Product kernel represents a paradigm shift in neural network design. Traditional approaches rely on dot-product operations followed by activation functions like ReLU:

\( y = \sigma(w \cdot x + b) \)

This architecture has fundamental limitations: anti-parallel vectors are penalized (-1), orthogonal vectors yield zero, and ReLU clipping causes information loss and uninterpretable entanglement.

The YAT-Product kernel unifies directional alignment and spatial proximity into a single, self-regulating operator:

\( K_{\text{ⵟ}}(w, x) = \frac{\langle w, x \rangle}{\|w - x\|^2 + \epsilon} \)

This gravitational Mercer kernel provides intrinsic non-linearity without requiring activation functions, eliminating the need for clipping operations and enabling fully interpretable latent spaces.
