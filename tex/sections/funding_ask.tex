\section{The Ask: \$1.59M Pre-Seed Round}

\subsection{Funding Request}

Azetta.ai is raising \textbf{\$1.59 million} in pre-seed funding to build and validate Cosma as the leading omnilingual semantic search platform. This capital will provide an \textbf{18-month runway} to achieve product-market fit and position the company for a successful Series A.

\textbf{Why \$1.59M?} Deep-tech AI startups face unique risks: GPU pricing volatility, extended enterprise sales cycles, and the need to attract senior ML talent in a competitive market. This raise provides the buffer to execute properly rather than running lean to the point of fragility.

\subsection{Key Milestones with This Funding}

The \$1.5M will enable Azetta.ai to achieve the following critical milestones over 18 months:

\subsubsection{Technical Milestones (Months 1-9)}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Q1 (Months 1-3)}: Production-ready CVD v1.0 with deterministic sub-50ms retrieval across 10M+ vectors
    
    \item \textbf{Q2 (Months 4-6)}: COEM multilingual models trained on 50+ languages with SOTA cross-lingual transfer performance
    
    \item \textbf{Q3 (Months 7-9)}: Cosma Search Layer (CSL) deployed with auto-scaling, multi-tenancy, and enterprise security (SOC 2 Type I)
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Commercial Milestones (Months 6-18)}

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Q2-Q3 (Months 6-9)}: 5-10 design partnerships across target verticals (e-commerce, SaaS, finance); establish product-market fit signals
    
    \item \textbf{Q4 (Months 10-12)}: Convert design partners to paying customers (5-10 customers); achieve \textbf{\$300K-350K ARR} from design partner conversions
    
    \item \textbf{Month 12 (End of Year 1)}: Reach \textbf{\$350K ARR} with 50-80 total customers (predominantly startups and SMBs, with 1-2 enterprise pilots)
    
    \item \textbf{Months 13-18}: Scale to \textbf{\$1.5M-2M ARR} run rate with 200-400 customers; begin enterprise sales motion with 6-9 month pipeline
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Why We're Conservative on Year 1:} Enterprise sales cycles are 9-15 months. Our Year 1 revenue comes from design partner conversions and SMB self-service—not enterprise deals. Enterprise ARR materializes in Year 2 once the pipeline matures.

\vspace{0.3cm}

These milestones are ambitious but achievable because of how we've architected both our product \textit{and} our business model for capital efficiency.

\subsection{Capital Efficiency Strategy}

Azetta.ai is optimizing burn rate through:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Lean Core Team}: Founders take below-market salaries (\$80K/year vs. \$150K+ market rate) to extend runway
    
    \item \textbf{Strategic Compute Spending}: Leverage spot instances and government research grants to reduce GPU costs by 40-60\%
    
    \item \textbf{Design Partner Model}: Early customers provide feedback and validation in exchange for discounted pricing, reducing CAC to near-zero for first 10 customers
    
    \item \textbf{Open-Source Leverage}: Release CVD core as open-source to drive developer adoption and community validation, reducing marketing costs
\end{itemize}

\vspace{0.3cm}

Beyond these operational efficiencies, our business model itself is designed to create structural advantages that reduce risk and accelerate growth.

\subsection{Business Model Moats: Built-In Defensibility}

Beyond product differentiation, Azetta.ai's pricing architecture creates \textbf{structural competitive advantages} that de-risk this investment:

\subsubsection{1. Self-Funding Growth Engine}

\textbf{Annual infrastructure commitments} mean customers prepay 12 months of cloud costs upfront:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item \textbf{Year 1}: \$105K in prepaid cash (30\% of \$350K ARR from design partner conversions)
    \item \textbf{Year 2}: \$1.5M in prepaid cash (30\% of \$5M ARR)
    \item \textbf{Year 3}: \$5.6M in prepaid cash (30\% of \$18.7M ARR)
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Cash Flow Assumption:} Our 18-month runway is calculated assuming \textbf{monthly billing} as the default. Annual prepayments provide upside acceleration but are not required for survival. This is critical: we do not depend on 100\% prepayment compliance—enterprise customers typically demand Net-30 or Net-60 terms.

\textbf{Investor impact}: By Year 3, we're generating \textbf{\$5.6M+ in upfront cash} annually—sufficient to extend runway and reduce Series B dependency.

\subsubsection{2. Competitive Immunity from At-Cost Pass-Through}

Our \textbf{zero-markup infrastructure pricing} creates an impossible-to-beat moat:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item Competitors charge 20-40\% markup on infrastructure (\$6K-7K for workloads we charge \$5K for)
    \item \textbf{They cannot undercut us}—matching our pricing destroys their margins
    \item We profit from high-margin intelligence (COEM models), not commodity infrastructure
    \item Enterprise buyers value radical transparency—builds trust and long-term partnerships
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Defensive positioning}: Competitors must either (1) match our transparency and lose margin, or (2) maintain markups and lose customers. We win either way.

\subsubsection{3. Expansion Revenue Built Into Architecture}

\textbf{Zero-fee scaling} (enabled by decoupled storage) drives organic expansion:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item Customers upgrade \textbf{without penalty}—eliminates friction that kills upsells at competitors
    \item Target: \textbf{110-130\% Net Revenue Retention (NRR)} by Year 3
    \item 20-30\% of customers pay usage overage monthly (automatic expansion revenue)
    \item Can downgrade during downturns without penalty—reduces churn risk to <5\%
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Math}: Every \$1 of new ARR becomes \$1.10-\$1.30 the following year through natural expansion. This compounds: \$1M ARR → \$1.1M → \$1.21M → \$1.33M over 3 years \textit{with zero additional sales effort}.

\subsubsection{4. Technical Moat = Pricing Moat}

Our decoupled architecture isn't just a technical feature—it's a \textbf{pricing differentiator competitors can't match}:

\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item Traditional vector databases couple storage + compute, forcing customers into rigid instance tiers
    \item We decouple storage, enabling zero-fee scaling up/down
\end{itemize}
